Tapir

Despite looking like a cross between a pig, an elephant and a panda, these natives of Southeast Asia are closely related to rhinos and horses. In fact, they are the only tapir species in Asia.

At Singapore Zoo, you can view our Malayan tapirs at home in their naturalistic habitat - dense lowland primary forests along the river bank. There are plenty of low-lying shrubs, soft twigs and succulent shoots and leaves for these herbivores to forage for and munch on.
